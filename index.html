Spreadsheets

It wasn’t like people were not doing calculations or making graphs or cross referencing data before the existence of digital spreadsheets, but the way spreadsheets have eased up these tasks for a user is amazing.


It has many attributes of a good software - it’s simple to start with, can go as deep as you want, and flexible in terms of what you wanna do with it, are a few superb qualities to start with.

Spreadsheets allow for

  • beginner stuff like SUM, AVG, MODE, SUMPRODUCT, IF, FILTERS, etc
  • intermediate stuff like LOOKUP, ROLLUP, PIVOT, CHART, etc
  • advanced stuff like creating a form within the spreadsheet, running complicated data analysis, creating forecasts and what-if scenarios, etc

I really love how they’ve put tremendous power in the hands of a user.


I cannot think of something that I would have liked to change with the approach that spreadsheets take on simplifying statistics and data operations for a user.
